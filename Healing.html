Health benefits from Sunchoke



Back to homepage

Sunchokes contain inulin, a prebiotic fiber. Inulin helps improve digestive health,

relieve constipation, promote weight loss, and helps to control diabetes. Studies have

proven that inulin can also provide heart health, mineral absorption, and even

colon cancer. Some of the most important minerals like, phosphorus, iron, and

calcium can also be found within sunchokes. We need phosphorus in our bodies to

keep our bones strong and healthy and to move our muscles, iron to make red blood

cells to carry oxygen to our body, as well as calcium to maintain strong bones and

to carry out many important body functions.
